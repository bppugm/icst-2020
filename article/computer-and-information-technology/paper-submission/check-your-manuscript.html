After you got the notification of acceptance, you need to re-check your paper format and content. This tutorial will guide you through analyzing your camera-ready manuscript.

Action 1
Check your paper format and content

The submitted camera-ready paper should be written according to the author guidelines.

The corresponding author have to check the title, list of authors, and their affiliations in the EDAS system and their paper. Any revision could not be made after uploading the camera-ready version by August 10.

Action 2
Analyze your camera-ready document

The submitted PDF document must have passed the PDF eXpress™ Plus. We highly recommend that you keep a backup of your DOC camera-ready document on your computer or another file storage.

PDF eXpress™ Plus is used to ensure the document are match with IEEE Xplore® compliance. The service is free of charge for ICST 2020 authors using Conference ID: 50505XP. Read how to use IEEE PDF eXpress™ .

For a new user, you need to create a new PDF eXpress™ account .
